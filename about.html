About us

The Hamilton Southeastern BEST Robotics team is filled with members from the Engineering Design and Development course. HSE competes in BEST Robotics yearly and have had lots of past successes winning multiple awards in the 2019 competition and winning the National competition in the 2018 competition. This year, we plan on continuing with our previous successes and hopefully make it to Nationals.

Company Bio

We are a team of college students working on this project like it's our full time job. Any amount would help support and continue development on this project and is greatly appreciated.
